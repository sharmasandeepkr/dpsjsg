MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL




“Remember those first steps that your child made while walking”


In an intensely competitive world today, there is an urgent need to start as early as possible, Doon Public School is the training centre for the citizens of tomorrow. What I appreciate most is the solid support and collaboration that we receive from the parents and well wishes in all our endeavors.

The faculty works collective with the student’s by providing practical inputs and insight is specific areas. The pedagogy followed by the teachers extends beyond the four walls of the classrooms. The entire learning process is an on- going fun filled journey, allowing student to amalgamate, assimilate and internalize academic inputs and information effortlessly. At Doon Public School we believe that refreshing environment contributes significantly towards the learning process by stimulating intellectual alertness and creativity as the institute is located in the sylvan surroundings distinguishable by serenity and calm.

I am confident that years at Doon Public School, Jharsuguda will transform you into a confident, knowledgeable and polite citizen who will never stop learning. I am sure this experience will enable you to develop a perspective and rational in working out things that will go a long way in creating values for you as well as others. In our constant effort and endless enthusiasm, I want your full co-operation through your support and feedback in parting quality education and serving our motto beyond the boundaries.
I wish all my students all the very best in their journey for excellence in everything you do in coming years.

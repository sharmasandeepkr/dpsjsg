About the school

“Education is the foundation of any Nation. The Nation grows only with Educating its individuals in Social and Economic reform.”

Doon Public School, Jharsuguda India’s Futuristic & High Tech School established on 12th February 2012, is affiliated to Doon Public School, New Delhi began its session on 1st April 2012 has seen it grow gradually at Jharsuguda. In this era of Globalization and Liberalization Doon Public School at Jharsuguda prepares its students to have a competitive edge over the others to meet the global perspectives with the best of the technology in education. The school enjoys a unique distinction of being the first school at Jharsuguda to have computer aided teaching and learning through Smart Classes. The digital Language Lab and the digital Library give the student to verify and visualize the concept of learning in the accessible concept. The up gradation programmes for teachers have enabled to achieve the highest quality of pedagogy. The programmed of social concern like the Mother’s Empowerment for Education development and Rural Educational Mission have brought a change in the life of the Local people. We provide the best of infrastructure soothed to our children. Our Motto is to server our children to enhance and their hidden qualities. Since it is in the initial stage we have to go miles & sky is limit.

The management of Doon Public School, Jharsuguda strongly believes in the Comprehensive System of Education that will help develop children into well grounded personalities and channelize their inherent talents.

The prefer amalgamation of the ‘Head & Heart’ is what we aim at, thus encouraging the spirit of enquiry and the teacher to be not only a friend, philosopher and guide but a facilitator or a co-learner, motivating studies to fulfill their dreams.

Doon Public School, Jharsuguda follows a well planned, graded and innovative curriculum based on the Central Board of Secondary Education and will endeavor to make learning a pleasurable experience. Being a dynamic Co-educational institution that believes in keeping pace with the changes in the field of education.

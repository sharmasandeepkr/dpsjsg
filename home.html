Doon Public School ranks amongst the top 10 senior secondary Public schools in Delhi/NCR. Life at Doon Public School centers on a shared commitment to academic excellence, intellectual growth and high standards of ethical awareness, sportsmanship, along with a sense of community service. The school’s renewed efforts to empower the students with a keen interest in arts and aesthetics, is reflected in the numerous extramural activities organized to add depth to each student’s life. The school's traditions and accessibility widen the spectrum of experiences for the child as well as the parent.
The school upholds the founders' commitment to excellence in all fields, to live up to its motto- The Excelsior; being better than the best!
